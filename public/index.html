About Qanaqa-e-Sabriya

Qanaqa-e-Sabriya is a spiritual center following the teachings and path of the great Sufi saint Hazrat Nizamuddin Aulia. Our mission is to spread love, peace, and spiritual wisdom.

Sayings of Nizamuddin Aulia

  • "The friends of God have no fear, nor do they grieve."
  • "The only way to achieve love of God is to love His creatures."
  • "Distance does not matter; only the relationship matters."

Kalams

Life of Nizamuddin Aulia

Hazrat Nizamuddin Aulia (1238-1325) was one of the most famous Sufi saints of the Indian subcontinent. His teachings emphasized love, compassion, and the oneness of humanity.

Born in Badayun, Uttar Pradesh, he later moved to Delhi where he spent most of his life. His shrine, known as Nizamuddin Dargah, is a prominent pilgrimage site.
